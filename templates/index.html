Things to remember

  • When adding riders, only add riders eligible for the omnium.
  • The system presumes riders will be entered in only one omnium.
  • It is required that each omnium rider have a unique USAC number. In the unlikely event of a day-of omnium registrant, work with Jason to create an unique identifier.
  • If omnium placings are edited manually, the individual race results will not reflect that change. Re-import the CSV or edit through the admin.
  • Before the race, double-check with Jason to ensure standard headers and field names.
